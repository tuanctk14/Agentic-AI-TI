Recon-Engine (subfinder, nmap, dnsx, whois):
The built-in reconnaissance engine performs network discovery including subdomain enumeration, port scanning, DNS resolution, and WHOIS lookups. These tools perform passive and active scanning that may be detected by target systems.
You must only scan systems that you own or have explicit written authorization to scan.

Unauthorized scanning of networks, domains, or IP addresses may violate federal and international laws including the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA), Computer Misuse Act, EU Directive 2013/40/EU, and equivalent legislation in your jurisdiction.

By default, recon-engine scans only domains registered as customer assets within your ArgusWatch instance. You are solely responsible for ensuring all scanned targets are within your authorized scope.
Threat Intelligence Collection: Intel-Proxy collects data from publicly accessible threat feeds and APIs. No unauthorized access is performed. All data sources are public OSINT feeds or API-key-authenticated services you have licensed.
